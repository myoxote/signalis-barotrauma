This is the GitHub repository for the SIGNALIS Replika Jobs mod for Barotrauma, uploaded to Steam Workshop.
Please note that this is a deprecated project, and that this repository exists that others might continue work on the mod.
The code is highkey very messy, and there may be incomplete parts in the repo. I would suggest using the STAR as a base, because it is currently the most "complete" Replika out of the ones available.

I won't be able to answer many questions about the mod because it's been a long while since I've last worked on it, but I sincerely appreciate the support and hope that there can be a complete version of the mod, for everyone's Replikamaxxing needs!!


Description:
Allows you to play as a Replika, a type of biomechanical android from the universe of SIGNALIS. You can access the Replikas via the job system at character select.

DISCLAIMER!!!
THIS MOD IS A HEAVY WORK-IN-PROGRESS. PLEASE EXPECT TO SEE A LOT OF BUGS, AND EXPECT THAT THIS MOD MAY CHANGE IN WAYS THAT AFFECT YOUR SAVES. UPDATE WHENEVER POSSIBLE FOR THE BEST EXPERIENCE!!!

I highly suggest not using Replikas as crew members, and only as player characters.

Requirements
Requires Lua for Barotrauma, and does not work in singleplayer at the current moment. Remember to enable the 'Lua for Barotrauma - DedicatedServer' server executable when playing multiplayer, or this mod will NOT WORK AS INTENDED!!

Features
Two types of playable Replikas are currently included in this mod, being the Starling (STAR) and the Storch (STCR) units. Additionally, STARs come with a custom talent tree, with plans to make a custom talent tree for each Replika that vaguely encompasses the standard facilities of a vanilla Barotrauma job. Storches do not have a custom talent tree at the moment.

For items, there are two types of custom body armor that these Replikas start with. They are functionally the same, but are cosmetically different (no masks for STARs yet, sorry).
Also, while taller Replikas can functionally fit in any suit, there is an unavoidable visual bug that occurs when they wear normal diving suits; the XL Diving Suit item was created to fix this. There will be other versions of this suit that more closely follow vanilla progress curves, but at the moment the current item can protect the wearer under 7000m.

All items should be craftable.

Planned Features
EULRs, ARARs, LSTRs, ADLRs, and KLBRs are planned, but at the moment not included in this package.

I also want to include a custom medical system revolving around repair sprays and repair patches.

At the moment, only English is supported. I will look into adding localization for other languages further down the line.

Bugs
Please comment if you encounter any bugs or issues. I offer no guarantees of a swift or feasible solution due to my limited modding capabilities. Additionally, there shouldn't be too many compatibility problems, but I am a novice modder and I will NOT be able to reliably or consistently tackle issues if they do arise.

At the moment the current known bugs/issues are:
- Replikas don't spawn with all the items they are supposed to.
- Replika jobs cannot access certain parts of the submarine that are limited to specific vanilla jobs like security (I think this may be hard coded or based on the submarine you are using, unfortunately).
- Replikas are not affected by + bonus skill effects (hard coded, probably). (<- NO LONGER AN ISSUE AS OF CALM BEFORE THE STORM UPDATE)
- Replikas very rarely spawn without hair.
- Replikas will spawn in the submarine and are registered as hostile crew members that will die and respawn??
- At the start of a round, it's possible for someone playing as a Replika to lose their entire inventory, skill sets, and revert back to the base items given when they spawn. KEEP YOUR ITEMS IN BACKUP CONTAINERS
